Abdallah and his friends

Once upon a time there was a little boy named Abdallah,Abdallah was a kind little boy but some of his friends didn't like him because they think of him as a small boy and so they didn't like playing football with him.Abdallah tried everything he could to blend in and play football with his friends but his effort was wasted uslesly so one day Abdallah had an idea to buy his ball and maybe the others may allow him play so he told his father to buy him a ball. After a day his father bought him the ball, when his father gave him the ball he thought that he should play with his ball by himself because he thought that if his friends see him playing they would be jealous and then ask him if they could play but then when Abdullah was about to go out his father told him that being stingy only leads to having less friends and then he told him to be always kind, then he went outside to play by himself because he thought that if he gave his friends they would burst his ball so that they can be mean to him but Abdallah remembered his fathers advice so he called all his friends to play with him after that his friends came to his house and apoligized for being rude and that they did that because they felt ashamed that if they play with people who are not their age they will lose their respect and the other people will look at them as the people who play with small children which are not their age and they will lose their dignity.After that Abdallah told them that they shouldn't worry and that he has forgiven them then his friends became nice, and then Abdallah's friends started playing football with him and they lived happily ever after.

Lessons learnt from this story

  1. It is not good to be stingy.
  2. Be nice to people whatever the situation.
  3. Never think you are above somebody just because you are better or stronger than them.
